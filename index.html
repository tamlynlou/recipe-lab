Cacio e Pepe

Ingredients

  • large amount of pasta
  • a chunk of butter
  • smashed garlic
  • LOTS of black pepper
  • dash salt
  • all of the parmesean

Method

Here we go!

  1. Boil water
  2. Cook water - keep some of that pasta water.
  3. Heat butter in separate pan. Toss in black pepper. Give it a couple of minutes, then add garlic.
  4. Add that pasta water and let simmer for a few.
  5. Remove pan from heat and toss that cooked pasta in. Once pasta is coated, add that grated cheese.
  6. Divide into bowls and top with a sprinkle more cheese!

All Done!
